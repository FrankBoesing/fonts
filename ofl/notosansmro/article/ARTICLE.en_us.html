Noto Sans Mro is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Indic Mro script.

Noto Sans Mro contains 48 glyphs, and supports 47 characters from the Unicode block Mro.

Supported writing systems

Mro

Mro (Mru, Murong) is an Indic alphabet, written left-to-right. Used in Bangladesh for the Mru language (30,000 speakers). Created in the 1980s by Menlay Murang (Manley Mro). Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
