Noto Sans MeeteiMayek is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Indic Meetei Mayek (Meitei) script.

Noto Sans MeeteiMayek has multiple weights, contains 92 glyphs, 2 OpenType features, and supports 87 characters from 2 Unicode blocks: Meetei Mayek, Meetei Mayek Extensions.

Supported writing systems

Meetei Mayek (Meitei)

Meetei Mayek (Meitei, ꯃꯤꯇꯩ ꯃꯌꯦꯛ) is an Indic abugida, written left-to-right. Used in India, Bangladesh, and Myanmar for the Meitei language (1.4 million users). Was used until the 18th century, then replaced by the Bengali script. Revived since the 1930s. Needs software support for complex text layout (shaping).
