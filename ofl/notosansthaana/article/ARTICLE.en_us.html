Noto Sans Thaana is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Indic Thaana script.

Noto Sans Thaana has multiple weights, contains 90 glyphs, and supports 89 characters from 4 Unicode blocks: Thaana, Basic Latin, Arabic, General Punctuation.

Supported writing systems

Thaana

Thaana (ދިވެހި) is an Indic alphabet, written right-to-left (350,000 users). Used on the Maldives and in India for the Maldivian (Mahl, Dhivehi) language, which also uses a Latin transliteration. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
