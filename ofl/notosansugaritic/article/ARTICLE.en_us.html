Noto Sans Ugaritic is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the historical Middle Eastern Ugaritic script.

Noto Sans Ugaritic contains 36 glyphs, and supports 35 characters from the Unicode block Ugaritic.

Supported writing systems

Ugaritic

Ugaritic is a historical Middle Eastern abjad, written left-to-right. Was used in today’s Syria in 1500-1300 BCE for the Ugaritic language, and also for Hurrian. Has 30 letters that visually resemble cuneiform. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
