Noto Sans Tai Le is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Southeast Asian Tai Le script.

Noto Sans Tai Le contains 71 glyphs, 2 OpenType features, and supports 64 characters from 3 Unicode blocks: Tai Le, CJK Symbols and Punctuation, Combining Diacritical Marks.

Supported writing systems

Tai Le

Tai Le (ᥖᥭᥰᥘᥫᥴ) is a Southeast Asian abugida, written left-to-right. Used in Yunnan, China since c. 1200 CE for the Tai Le (Tai Nüa) language. Revised several times in 1952–1988. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
