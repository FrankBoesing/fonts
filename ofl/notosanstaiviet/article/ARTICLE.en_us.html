Noto Sans Tai Viet is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Southeast Asian Tai Viet script.

Noto Sans Tai Viet contains 83 glyphs, and supports 82 characters from the Unicode block Tai Viet.

Supported writing systems

Tai Viet

Tai Viet is a Southeast Asian abugida, written left-to-right. Used since the 16th century in Vietnam, Laos, China and Thailand for the Tai Dam, Tai Dón, Tai Daeng, Thai Song and Tày Tac languages. Has 31 consonants and 14 vowels. Needs software support for complex text layout (shaping). Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
