Noto Sans Linear A is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the historical European Linear A script.

Noto Sans Linear A contains 346 glyphs, and supports 345 characters from the Unicode block Linear A.

Supported writing systems

Linear A

Linear A is a historical undeciphered European logo-syllabary, written left-to-right. Was used 1800-1450 BCE in ancient Crete, alongside Cretan Hieroglyphs, for the hypothesized Minoan language. Succeeded by Linear B. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
