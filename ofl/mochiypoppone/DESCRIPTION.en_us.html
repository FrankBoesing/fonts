Mochiy Pop is a casual Gothic font created based on characters written by girls in their 20s in Japan. A cute display font, you can use it widely, such as for manga, magazines, movies, and signs. The included Kanji are modified versions of Noto Sans JP.

Mochiy Pop P includes proportional-wdith kana. Mochiy Pop (https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Mochiy+Pop) includes full-width kana.

To contribute to the project, visit github.com/fontdasu/Mochiypop
