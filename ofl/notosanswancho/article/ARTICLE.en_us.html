Noto Sans Wancho is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Indic Wancho script.

Noto Sans Wancho contains 95 glyphs, 3 OpenType features, and supports 79 characters from 2 Unicode blocks: Wancho, Basic Latin.

Supported writing systems

Wancho

Wancho is an Indic alphabet, written left-to-right. Created 2001–2012 by Banwang Losu in India for the Wancho language. Some schools teach the Wancho script but the language generally uses Devanagari and Latin script. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, r12a.
