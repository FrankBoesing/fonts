Noto Sans Tifinagh is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the African Tifinagh script.

Noto Sans Tifinagh contains 164 glyphs, 5 OpenType features, and supports 76 characters from 2 Unicode blocks: Tifinagh, Combining Diacritical Marks.

Supported writing systems

Tifinagh

Tifinagh (ⵜⵉⴼⵉⵏⴰⵖ) is an African abjad. Used alongside the Berber Latin Alphabet for Berber languages of North Africa (1 million speakers), and for Tuareg languages. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
