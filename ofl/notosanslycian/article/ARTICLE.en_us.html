Noto Sans Lycian is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the historical European Lycian script.

Noto Sans Lycian contains 34 glyphs, and supports 33 characters from the Unicode block Lycian.

Supported writing systems

Lycian

Lycian is a historical European alphabet, written left-to-right. Was used 500-330 BCE in today’s southern Turkey for the Lycian language. Has 29 letters, visually similar to archaic Greek. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
