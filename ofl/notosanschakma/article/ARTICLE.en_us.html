Noto Sans Chakma is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Indic Chakma script.

Noto Sans Chakma contains 212 glyphs, 12 OpenType features, and supports 97 characters from the Unicode block Chakma.

Supported writing systems

Chakma

Chakma (Ojhapath, Ojhopath, Ajhapath, 𑄌𑄋𑄴𑄟𑄳𑄦 𑄃𑄧𑄏𑄛𑄖𑄴) is an Indic abugida, written left-to-right (170,000 users). Used in Bangladesh and India for the Chakma language, and for Tanchangya in Bangladesh. Brahmic script related to Mon Khmer and Myanmar. Needs software support for complex text layout (shaping).
