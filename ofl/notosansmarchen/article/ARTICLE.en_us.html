Noto Sans Marchen is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the historical Indic Marchen script.

Noto Sans Marchen contains 748 glyphs, 6 OpenType features, and supports 73 characters from the Unicode block Marchen.

Supported writing systems

Marchen

Marchen is a historical Indic abugida, written left-to-right. Marchen (Greater Mar) was used by followers of the Tibetan Bön religion for writing the Zhang-zhung language. Needs software support for complex text layout (shaping). Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
