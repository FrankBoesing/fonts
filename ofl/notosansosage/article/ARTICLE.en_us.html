Noto Sans Osage is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the American Osage script.

Noto Sans Osage contains 82 glyphs, 2 OpenType features, and supports 81 characters from 2 Unicode blocks: Osage, Combining Diacritical Marks.

Supported writing systems

Osage

Osage is an American bicameral alphabet, written left-to-right. Used in the USA for the revitalized native Osage language. Derived from Latin 2006–2014 by Herman Mongrain Lookout. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
