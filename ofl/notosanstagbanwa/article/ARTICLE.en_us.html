Noto Sans Tagbanwa is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Southeast Asian Tagbanwa script.

Noto Sans Tagbanwa contains 29 glyphs, and supports 28 characters from the Unicode block Tagbanwa.

Supported writing systems

Tagbanwa

Tagbanwa (ᝦᝪᝯ) is a Southeast Asian abugida, written left-to-right. Used in the Philippines since c. 1300 for the Tagbanwa language (8–25,000 speakers). Has 13 consontants. The script and language are in decline, being replaced by Tagalog. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
