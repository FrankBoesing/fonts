Noto Sans Osmanya is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the historical African Osmanya script.

Noto Sans Osmanya contains 45 glyphs, and supports 44 characters from the Unicode block Osmanya.

Supported writing systems

Osmanya

Osmanya (Far Soomaali, Farta Cismaanya, 𐒍𐒖𐒇𐒂𐒖 𐒋𐒘𐒈𐒑𐒛𐒒𐒕𐒖) is a historical African alphabet, written left-to-right. Was sporadically used 1922-1973 for writing the Somali language. Created by Cusmaan Yuusuf Keenadiid. Almost fully replaced by the Latin script in 1973. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
