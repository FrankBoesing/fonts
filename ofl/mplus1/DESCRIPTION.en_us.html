M+ FONTS is a little nifty font family for everyday usage. Mplus 1 is a Sans Serif font with nine weights from Thin to Black, supporting 5,700+ Kanjis for Japanese with GF Latin Plus. With the harmony of comfortable curves and straight lines, this font gives modern and generous impression, suiting for any occasions including small texts to big titles.

To contribute to the project, visit https://github.com/coz-m/MPLUS_FONTS
